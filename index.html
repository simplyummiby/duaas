About This App

Duʿā Companion is a calm space for morning, evening, and before-sleep remembrance.

About Duʿā Companion

This app is designed to help you keep a gentle routine of remembrance without turning worship into pressure or noise.

Habit tracking: Morning, Evening, and Before Sleep count toward the habit calendar when you recite at least one duʿā from that collection.

Note: Daily duʿās are not included in this version.

How it works

  • Morning, Evening, and Before Sleep count toward habits.
  • Reciting even one from a collection counts for that day.
  • Today's checked items reset each day; your calendar remains.
