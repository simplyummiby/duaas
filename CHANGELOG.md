# Changelog

## v1.2
- Replaced Collections navigation with a Resources page organized into General Duʿā, Morning & Evening Adhkār, and Before Sleep sections.
- Updated navigation to Home, Morning Adhkār, Evening Adhkār, Before Sleep, Resources, Backup & Restore, and About.
- Clarified collection pages with hero areas, purpose statements, Today’s Progress, collection habit calendars, and clickable “Open →” duʿā rows.
- Reworked Focus Mode as a full-screen, single-scroll reading experience with fixed Previous, Complete / Undo Complete, and Next controls.
- Kept habit tracking limited to Morning, Evening, and Before Sleep, with daily item progress separated from persistent habit calendar history.
- Refined Backup & Restore and About copy for localStorage progress and backup reminders.
